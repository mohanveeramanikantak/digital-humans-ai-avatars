# 🤖 Digital Humans & AI Avatars

## 📌 Overview

Digital Humans and AI Avatars are intelligent virtual characters powered by artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing.

These systems are designed to simulate human interaction, emotions, communication, and behavior in virtual environments.

---

## 🔮 Future Vision

* Human-like AI assistants
* Virtual influencers and presenters
* AI customer support avatars
* Realistic metaverse characters
* Emotion-aware digital companions

---

## ⚙️ Core Technologies

### 1. Artificial Intelligence

Enables intelligent conversations and decision-making.

### 2. Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Allows avatars to understand and respond to human language.

### 3. Computer Vision

Supports facial recognition and emotion detection.

### 4. 3D Animation

Creates realistic avatar appearance and movement.
